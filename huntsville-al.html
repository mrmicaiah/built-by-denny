VA SAH GRANT CONTRACTOR IN HUNTSVILLE, ALABAMA

As the largest city in North Alabama, Huntsville is home to thousands of veterans—many with service-connected disabilities who deserve accessible housing. Built by Denny is proud to be Huntsville's trusted VA SAH Grant contractor, helping disabled veterans build or modify their homes for complete wheelchair accessibility.

With Redstone Arsenal nearby and a strong military community, we understand the unique needs of Huntsville's veteran population. Whether you need a brand new wheelchair accessible home or modifications to your existing home, we handle everything from VA paperwork to final inspection.

Our Huntsville Services Include:

  • Wheelchair accessible new home construction - Custom homes designed for full mobility
  • Roll-in shower installation - Zero-threshold showers for safe, independent bathing
  • Wheelchair ramp construction - Custom ramps with proper slopes and handrails
  • ADA bathroom modifications - Grab bars, roll-under sinks, comfort-height toilets
  • Accessible kitchen remodeling - Lowered counters, pull-out shelving, roll-under prep areas
  • Doorway widening - 36"+ clearance throughout your home
