What We Do

OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive accessible home construction and modification services for disabled veterans throughout North Alabama.

CUSTOM ACCESSIBLE HOME CONSTRUCTION

We build new homes from the ground up, designed specifically for wheelchair accessibility and your unique needs. Every aspect of the home is planned with mobility in mind, ensuring you have complete independence in your own space.

  • Open floor plans optimized for wheelchair navigation
  • Extra-wide hallways and doorways (36"+ clearance)
  • Single-story designs or elevator-ready construction
  • Reinforced walls for future grab bar installation
  • Accessible garage with proper clearances
  • Smart home technology integration

ACCESSIBLE BATHROOM MODIFICATIONS

The bathroom is often the most challenging room for wheelchair users. We transform bathrooms into safe, functional spaces that allow for complete independence while maintaining a beautiful, modern design.

  • Roll-in showers with zero-threshold entry
  • Fold-down shower benches and transfer seats
  • Strategically placed grab bars
  • Comfort-height toilets with support rails
  • Roll-under vanities and sinks
  • Anti-slip flooring throughout
  • Handheld showerheads on adjustable slides

ACCESSIBLE KITCHEN DESIGN

We create kitchens where every cabinet, appliance, and work surface is within reach. Our accessible kitchen designs combine functionality with style, making cooking and meal prep enjoyable again.

  • Lowered countertops and work surfaces
  • Roll-under sinks and prep areas
  • Pull-out shelving and lazy susans
  • Side-opening ovens and accessible microwaves
  • Touch-activated faucets
  • Drawer-style dishwashers and refrigerators

WHEELCHAIR RAMPS & ACCESSIBLE ENTRIES

Getting in and out of your home should be effortless. We design and build ramps and entry modifications that are both functional and attractive, complementing your home's architecture.

  • Custom wooden or concrete ramps
  • Proper slope ratios (1:12 or better)
  • Non-slip surfaces and handrails
  • Covered ramp options for weather protection
  • Zero-step entries and thresholds
  • Automatic door openers
  • Widened doorways and lever handles

WHOLE-HOME ACCESSIBILITY MODIFICATIONS

Sometimes a complete home transformation is needed. We can modify your existing home to meet full ADA accessibility standards, room by room, ensuring every space works for you.

  • Doorway widening throughout the home
  • Flooring transitions and threshold removal
  • Bedroom modifications and closet access
  • Laundry room accessibility
  • Stairlifts or residential elevators
  • Lighting and electrical modifications
  • Emergency egress planning

USING YOUR VA SAH GRANT?

We specialize in VA Specially Adapted Housing grant projects. We'll handle the paperwork, coordinate with the VA, and ensure your project meets all requirements.
