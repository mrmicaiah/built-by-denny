ACCESSIBLE HOME SERVICES

Wheelchair accessible home construction, roll-in showers, ramps, and ADA modifications for disabled veterans throughout North Alabama.

WHEELCHAIR ACCESSIBLE NEW HOME CONSTRUCTION

We build custom homes from the ground up, designed specifically for wheelchair accessibility and independent living. Every aspect of your new home is planned with mobility in mind, ensuring complete freedom in your own space.

  • Open floor plans optimized for wheelchair navigation
  • Extra-wide hallways and doorways (36"+ clearance)
  • Single-story designs or elevator-ready construction
  • Reinforced walls for future grab bar installation
  • Accessible garage with proper clearances
  • Smart home technology integration for accessibility

Perfect for: Veterans using VA SAH Grants to build a new accessible home in Huntsville, Decatur, Athens, Florence, or anywhere in North Alabama.

ROLL-IN SHOWERS & ACCESSIBLE BATHROOMS

The bathroom is often the most challenging room for wheelchair users. We specialize in roll-in shower installation and complete accessible bathroom remodels that allow for safe, independent bathing.

  • Roll-in showers with zero-threshold entry
  • Fold-down shower benches and transfer seats
  • Strategically placed grab bars and safety rails
  • Comfort-height toilets with support rails
  • Roll-under vanities and sinks
  • Anti-slip flooring throughout
  • Handheld showerheads on adjustable slides

Keywords we serve: Roll-in shower installation Huntsville AL, accessible bathroom remodel Decatur, ADA bathroom contractor North Alabama.

WHEELCHAIR RAMPS & ACCESSIBLE ENTRIES

Getting in and out of your home should be effortless. We design and build custom wheelchair ramps and entry modifications that are both functional and attractive, complementing your home's architecture.

  • Custom wooden or concrete wheelchair ramps
  • Proper slope ratios (1:12 or better) for easy navigation
  • Non-slip surfaces and sturdy handrails
  • Covered ramp options for weather protection
  • Zero-step entries and threshold elimination
  • Automatic door openers
  • Widened doorways (36" minimum) and lever handles

Keywords we serve: Wheelchair ramp contractor Huntsville AL, accessible entry modifications Alabama, zero-step entry installation.

ADA ACCESSIBLE KITCHEN MODIFICATIONS

We create kitchens where every cabinet, appliance, and work surface is within reach from a wheelchair. Our accessible kitchen designs combine functionality with style, making cooking and meal prep enjoyable and independent.

  • Lowered countertops and work surfaces
  • Roll-under sinks and prep areas
  • Pull-out shelving and lazy susans for easy access
  • Side-opening ovens and accessible microwaves
  • Touch-activated faucets
  • Drawer-style dishwashers and refrigerators
  • Adequate knee clearance throughout

Keywords we serve: ADA kitchen modifications Huntsville, accessible kitchen remodel Alabama, wheelchair accessible kitchen contractor.

WHOLE-HOME ACCESSIBILITY MODIFICATIONS

Sometimes a complete home transformation is needed. We can modify your existing home to meet full ADA accessibility standards, room by room, ensuring every space works for wheelchair users and those with mobility challenges.

  • Doorway widening throughout the home
  • Flooring transitions and threshold removal
  • Bedroom modifications and accessible closets
  • Laundry room accessibility
  • Stairlifts or residential elevator installation
  • Lighting and electrical modifications
  • Emergency egress planning

Keywords we serve: Disability home modifications North Alabama, ADA home compliance contractor, whole home accessibility remodel.
