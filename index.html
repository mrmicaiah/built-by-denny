Our Specialty

VA SAH GRANT HOME CONSTRUCTION

We specialize in building specially adapted homes for veterans with service-connected disabilities, utilizing VA SAH grant funding to create accessible living spaces.

WHEELCHAIR ACCESSIBILITY

Wide doorways, roll-under counters, accessible bathrooms, and ramp systems designed to maximize independence and mobility throughout your home.

CUSTOM FLOOR PLANS

Every home is designed around your specific needs and the requirements of your VA grant, ensuring maximum functionality and comfort.

VA GRANT NAVIGATION

We understand the SAH grant process and work directly with the VA to ensure your project meets all requirements for maximum benefit.

ROLL-IN SHOWERS

Barrier-free bathroom designs with grab bars, adjustable fixtures, and non-slip surfaces for safe, independent bathing.

SINGLE-LEVEL LIVING

Open concept designs that eliminate stairs and maximize accessible living space on a single floor.

SMART HOME FEATURES

Voice-activated controls, automated lighting, and smart home systems that increase independence for daily living.
