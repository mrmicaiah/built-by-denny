How It Works

YOUR PATH TO AN ACCESSIBLE HOME

The VA SAH grant process can feel overwhelming. We're here to guide you through each step, from initial eligibility to handing you the keys.

1. DETERMINE ELIGIBILITY

The VA SAH grant is available to veterans with qualifying service-connected disabilities.

  • Loss or loss of use of both legs
  • Loss or loss of use of both arms
  • Blindness plus loss of use of a leg
  • Severe burns
  • ALS (any stage)
1

2. APPLY FOR YOUR GRANT

Submit VA Form 26-4555 to apply. The VA will review and issue your Certificate of Eligibility (COE).

  • Complete VA Form 26-4555
  • Provide medical documentation
  • Receive your COE
  • Current grant max: ~$109,986
