VA SAH GRANT CONTRACTOR IN NEW HOPE, ALABAMA

New Hope offers a peaceful, rural setting for veterans looking to build an accessible home. Built by Denny is your trusted VA SAH Grant contractor in New Hope, specializing in wheelchair accessible home construction and modifications.

Located in eastern Madison County near the foothills, New Hope provides an ideal setting for veterans who want space and tranquility. We help disabled veterans build or modify their homes for complete wheelchair accessibility, handling everything from VA paperwork to final construction.

Our New Hope Services Include:

  • Wheelchair accessible new home construction - Custom homes on your land
  • Roll-in shower installation - Zero-threshold showers for safe bathing
  • Wheelchair ramp construction - Custom ramps with proper ADA slopes
  • ADA bathroom modifications - Grab bars, roll-under sinks, accessible fixtures
  • Accessible kitchen remodeling - Lowered counters, pull-out shelving
  • Rural property accessibility - Pathways, outdoor access solutions
